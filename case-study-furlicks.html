FURLICKS - Only the best for our best friends.

Brand Strategy, Content Strategy, Social Media Launch

FMCG-WELLNESS

Overview-

Furlicks set out to make pet nutrition easier with orally dissolving supplements - because giving a pill to a dog or cat is never as simple as it sounds. We worked on building the brand from the ground up, including branding, packaging, social media, photoshoots, an overarching brand guide, and outdoor collateral.

We started with the identity: understanding what builds trust between a pet parent and a new brand. Our research showed that families respond to warmth, clarity, and playfulnessâ€”so the visual direction leaned into bright colours, approachable fonts, and a distinctive fur-like texture that became the hero element.

Execution-

For Instagram, we worked on content buckets that covered pet problems, product benefits, daily scenarios, and funny memes 'cause hey, who doesn't love a puppy meme? We obviously had to play outside too, so we made things happen with custom merchandise, and print collaterals for an outdoor event setup.

With this one, we put all paws on deck to shape up a new era of playful pet nutrition.
